PM Agent skill validation and quality assurance for product management capabilities

## Validation Rules

### 1. PM Skill Structure Validation
#### Required PM Skills
- Core PM skills: planning, requirement definition, analytics
- Strategic PM skills: roadmap, growth, launch, global strategy
- Enhanced PM skills: lifecycle, risk, coordination, retention
- Modern PM skills: data-driven, user research
- Stakeholder management skills
- Market research skills

#### Skill Level Requirements
