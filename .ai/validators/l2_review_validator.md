# Senior Review Validator

## Purpose
- Senior-level work validation and verification
- Senior skill competency assessment
- Cross-agent quality assurance

## Validation Criteria
### Technical Excellence
- Architecture design quality
- Strategic decision validation
- Complex problem-solving approaches
- Innovation and optimization levels

### Leadership & Mentorship
- Knowledge transfer effectiveness
- Team guidance quality
- Decision-making authority
- Cross-functional coordination

### Business Impact
- Strategic value creation
- Risk management effectiveness
- Resource optimization
- Long-term planning quality

## Validation Process
1. Senior work submission review
2. Technical complexity assessment
3. Business impact evaluation
4. Leadership competency verification
5. Cross-agent integration check
6. Quality standards compliance

## Integration
- Link to agent Senior skill matrices
- Connect to HR mentorship programs
- Integrate with existing validators
- Align with workflow systems
