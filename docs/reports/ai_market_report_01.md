# Competitor Market Scan Report

## Scope & Criteria

This report identifies competitor companies that provide services or products delivering structured internal documentation as an output. A company qualifies as a competitor ONLY if it satisfies ALL of the following criteria:

- Provides structured outputs such as internal documents, operational policies, decision logs, or change history records
- Delivers results, systems, or managed outputs (not just tools, and not pure consulting)
- Has evidence of activity within the last 12 months

**Exclusions:**
- Legal, tax, accounting, or compliance-specialized firms
- Pure consulting firms without defined deliverables
- Companies describing internal problems only (customers, not competitors)
- Inactive, closed, or abandoned services
