# Senior Decision Validator

## Purpose
- Strategic decision quality validation
- Senior level decision-making assessment
- High-stakes choice verification

## Validation Criteria
### Strategic Alignment
- Business goal consistency
- Long-term vision alignment
- Risk-reward balance assessment
- Resource optimization evaluation

### Decision Quality
- Analysis completeness
- Alternative consideration
- Stakeholder impact assessment
- Implementation feasibility

### Leadership Competence
- Decision-making authority
- Cross-functional coordination
- Risk management capability
- Communication effectiveness

## Validation Process
1. Decision context analysis
2. Strategic alignment verification
3. Analysis completeness check
4. Risk assessment validation
5. Implementation planning review
6. Stakeholder impact evaluation

## Agent-Specific Decision Validation
### Developer Senior Decisions
- Architecture choices
- Technology stack decisions
- Technical debt management
- Performance optimization strategies

### PM Senior Decisions
- Product roadmap priorities
- Market positioning strategies
- Resource allocation decisions
- Go-to-market approaches

### Finance Senior Decisions
- Investment portfolio strategies
- Risk management approaches
- Financial planning decisions
- Cost optimization initiatives

### Contents-Creator Senior Decisions
- Contents strategy directions
- Brand guideline establishment
- Platform partnership choices
- Revenue model selections

## Integration
- Connect to Senior skill matrices
- Link to business strategy validation
- Integrate with risk assessment systems
- Align with governance frameworks
